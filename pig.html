Short Description

The pig comes last on the 12-year cycle of animals which appear in the Chinese zodiac related to the Chinese calendar.
Lucky Colors: yellow, gray, brown, gold Lucky Numbers: 2,5,8
Lucky Flowers: hydrangea, daisy Years of Birth: 1923, 1935, 1947, 1959, 1971, 1983, 1995, 2007, 2019, 2031
Best with: Goat or Rabbit Worst with: Snake, Pig or Monkey

Personalities

They have such traits as diligence, sympathy, and generosity.
With great concentration, once set a goal, they will devote all energy to achieve the goal. Though pigs rarely seek help from others, they will not refuse to give a hand to others.
They never suspect someone else, so they are easily fooled by others. If one wants to have a long-term relationship with pigs, one should be honest with them and have a knowledge of their inner thoughts.
General speaking, pigs are relatively calm when facing trouble. No matter how difficult the problems are they encounter, they can handle things properly with carefulness.
They have a great sense of responsibility to finish what they are engaged in.
